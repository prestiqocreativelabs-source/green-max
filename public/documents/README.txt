DOCUMENT UPLOAD INSTRUCTIONS
============================

To add your company documents for the Certifications section:

1. Scan your documents as PDF or high-quality image files (JPG/PNG)

2. Rename them according to these filenames:
   - company-registration.pdf (or .jpg/.png)
   - gst-registration.pdf (or .jpg/.png)
   - pan-card.pdf (or .jpg/.png)
   - financial-info.pdf (or .jpg/.png)

3. Place the files in this folder: public/documents/

4. The documents will be viewable in the Certifications section when users click on them

5. Documents open in a viewer modal - users can view but not download them directly

Note: For security, you may want to add watermarks to your documents before uploading.
